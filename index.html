Stephanie Richards

Stephanie Richards is a data analyst with a passion for telling stories. She is curious about a wide variety of topics, from public health to social justice to conservation and the environment. She enjoys research, problem-solving, and asking big questions, and then digging in to finding the answers. She is detail-oriented and thorough, looking at problems from many different angles and perspectives until she is satisfied that she has found the best answers possible.

Prior to entering the world of data, Stephanie worked as a stagehand at the Guthrie Theater in Minneapolis for almost 20 years. As a technician and theater professional, she honed her skills in troubleshooting, problem-solving, leadership, and the ability to work well independently and also to collaborate with teams to product the best product possible.
